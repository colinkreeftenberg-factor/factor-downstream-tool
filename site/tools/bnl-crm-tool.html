Swap & Shortage Export Tool
Made & maintained by Colin Kreeftenberg — questions, edits or new templates go to him.
Welcome aboard! This is where messy dashboard exports get turned into clean, FileZilla-ready sendout files — upload, check, download, done.
01 Upload the dashboard export
CSV with one row per SKU per impacted order (customer_id, order_item_id, locale, comment, Recipe_Swapped, Recipe_Swapped_to, …). Grab it from the dashboard export sheet.
02 Process
Matches locale + reason to the comms templates. Multiple impacted meals get merged into one message, customers with both a swap and a shortage get one combined email, and swaps missing their replacement data get the generic fallback.
03 Review
Swaps vs. shorts per locale, how many customers had more than one impacted meal, and what those merged messages actually look like.
04 Download
One unicode text per locale in UTF-8. Customers needing a manual look are excluded from these and require manual processing.
05 Slack update
A short summary to post in the ops channel once the files are imported.
Factor · internal tool · content sourced from the backend comms sheet — update the tool if wording changes.
