Directs unavoidable swaps and shortages to the right customers with the right substitutes, then generates the compensation file and the customer emails in one pass.
No zone or meal-choice data yet, so impact spreads evenly across the run instead. Protein and allergens come from the Meal Database when a recipe matches — otherwise it's a flagged guess.
